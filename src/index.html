About

What is ProfesSearch?

One-stop for all research interns across the globe. A platform to search for the best place for your research interests to bud up.

Why ProfesSearch?

Process involved in searching for a research internship
  • Find your interest
  • Find professors of your interest in esteemed Universities
  • Make a database of these professors
  • Mail them one by one
  • Give an interview to that professor who replied positive.
Where we come in?

Searching for professors and making database are the most boring and time-consuming steps

Going through multiple websites. Searching through separate pages for each professor.

Which becomes hastier if your field of interest is multi-disciplinary.

So here we are to save your time by providing you with the collection of all information you need.
